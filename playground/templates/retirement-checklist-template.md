## Retirement Reasons

- [ ] The hypothesis did not prove valuable enough to continue.
- [ ] The experiment cannot be made bounded enough for meaningful progression.
- [ ] The experiment duplicates a better approach already under evaluation.
- [ ] The experiment depends on assumptions that no longer fit Calcwiz direction.

## Cleanup Check

- [ ] The experiment record explains why it is being retired.
- [ ] The records index is updated.
- [ ] The manifest status is updated if a manifest exists.
- [ ] No stable product code depends on the experiment.
